Who Are We

Our company All Asia Overseas Pvt. Ltd. is one fo the leading Government approved Manpower Agency License No: 1305/074/075 in Nepal registered under the Ministry of Labour & Overseas Employement to provide a proper channel for the origanized outflow of Nepalese Labour force abroad. All Asia Overseas is a renowed and successfully established company in Nepal.
